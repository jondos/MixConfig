Next/Previous Mix and Proxy Panel

This panel contains information about one of your neighbour servers.

Previous/Next Mix Certificate

The certificates are needed
  • Import
    Reads a certificate that you have received from a neighbour mix from a file. After you imported it, you should check whether the certificate's hash value is the same as the value that the other mix' operator told you. Otherwise some attacker might have replaced this certificate by his own.
  • Export
    Saves the certificate to a file.
  • Remove
    Deletes the certificate. Please make sure you either have a backup of the certificate of you really don't need it any more.

Location

This is the location of the neighbour Mix. It is certified by the neighbour Mix operator.

Operator

Here you find information about your neighbour operator. It is checked by a certification authority.

Next Mix

If you are running a middle Mix, please enter here the connection data (IP address / host name and port) of your next neighbout Mix. The port is often 6544.

Proxy

If you are running a last Mix, please enter here the information about your cache proxies and/or SOCKS proxies. You have to enter one cache/HTTP proxy at east. If you have locally installed a squid proxy, this is usually host=localhost and port=3128.

If ou are using dante as SOCKS proxy, this is usually host=localhost and port=1080.

You may enter as many proxies as you want.
